Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

ClassNotes ("I", "us", "we", or "our") operates the ClassNotes website (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of data when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect specific information to provide and improve the Service, specifically for our upload features:

  • Contributor Accounts: If you register to upload notes, we collect your Email Address to verify your identity and manage your upload privileges. This is stored securely in our database.
  • Usage Data: We collect pseudonymous usage data (such as document views, and downloads) linked to a temporary unique identifier to understand content popularity and improve the Service.
  • Comments: If you leave a comment, we store the text and the name you provide (if any).

2. How We Store Your Data

  • Local Storage: We use Local Storage to save your preferences (Theme, Semester, and Favorites) on your device. This data remains on your device and is not sent to our servers.
  • Database: User metadata (verification status, email) is stored in our Databases for user registered to upload notes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your eligibility to upload content (preventing spam).
  • To attribute uploaded notes to the correct contributor (internally).
  • To respond to support requests or takedown notices.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may disclose information if:

  • Required by Law: We are compelled to do so by a subpoena or court order.
  • Violation of Terms: A user repeatedly uploads infringing content, we may use their email to block access.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your account and data. Please contact us at notes@alokdasofficial.in.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at notes@alokdasofficial.in.
